# GCSE Science Made Easy 🚀

Free open-source GCSE Biology, Chemistry, and Physics revision resources for students worldwide.

---

## 📚 What this project includes:
- Detailed revision notes  
- Topic-wise summaries  
- Exam-style questions  
- Worksheets with answers  

---

## 🌍 Impact:
Helping 500+ GCSE students through online communities and WhatsApp study groups.

---

## 🎯 Mission:
To make high-quality GCSE science education accessible to every student, regardless of their background.

---

## 📂 Subjects Covered:
- Biology  
- Chemistry  
- Physics  

---

## 🔄 Updates:
New resources are added regularly based on student needs and exam trends.

---

## 🤝 Contributions:
This is an open project. Suggestions and improvements are always welcome.
